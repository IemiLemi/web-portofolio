About me

Hello. I am Nuță Emilia-Maria, a student at the Faculty of Economics and Business Administration at Babeș-Bolyai University. My interest in web development begun in middle school with Tumblr and MyAnimeList, when I tried to change the themes — thus having my first interaction with HTML and CSS!

Altought I took a long break after that, now I study Business Information System and I try to improve myself in this field of study.

My main focus right now is to get better at building accessible and inclusive digital products.
